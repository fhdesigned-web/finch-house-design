About

We combine strong planning, technical accuracy and editorial taste to create spaces that feel harmonious, comfortable and truly yours — and look stylish without template solutions.

Anastasia Finch

Architectural Planner & Interior Designer

Anastasia’s first degree is in medicine — she worked as a doctor and audiologist. That background shaped a very practical design mindset: real comfort, real routines, and spaces that work for everyday life. She focuses on planning, ergonomics, and technical clarity — from smart layouts and storage logic to clean drawing sets and reliable specifications. Strong in Archicad (and beyond), Anastasia ensures each project is functional, calm, and beautifully resolved.

Natalia Orudzheva

Interior Design, Decor & Styling Expert

Natalia’s first degree is in writing, and her editorial background shows in every project: coherent concepts, strong visual logic, and a sharp eye for details. She has worked with international design media (including Domus, Salon Interior, Wallpaper*, Harper’s Bazaar and others), and brings deep trend research into practical choices — finishes, decor, and styling that feel current but not disposable. Natalia turns complex ideas into clear, confident direction, so clients understand the concept and can implement it without stress.

Comfort isn’t a luxury. We design harmony tailored to you — so everyday life feels lighter. Remote, worldwide.
