Process

A clear, remote-first workflow. You always know what happens next, what you’ll receive, and what we need from you.

Typical timeline

Most projects take 7–14 business days after we receive your brief and measurements. Faster timelines are possible depending on scope and availability.

How we work

  • Remote-first: video call + messaging, with clear checkpoints.
  • We keep decisions structured: layout first, then finishes, then shopping/spec.
  • You get deliverables you can actually use (not a moodboard-only story).

What we need from you

Even a short message is enough to start. The more precise the inputs, the faster and cleaner the result.

  • Plan or sketch with key measurements (or a quick video + dimensions).
  • Photos in daylight (and evening if possible).
  • Your goal, budget range, and timeline.
  • Anything that must stay (furniture, finishes, constraints).
Send a brief

Step-by-step

Step 1

Brief + scope

We clarify goals, constraints, budget, and timeline. Then we confirm the best package and a clear quote.

  • You send: plan/photos + priorities.
  • We deliver: scope, timeline, quote, next steps.
Step 2

Layout + planning logic

We build a comfort-first layout: zones, flow, furniture placement, and storage logic. This step prevents expensive mistakes later.

  • You review: one structured revision round included.
  • We deliver: final layout + clear planning notes.
Step 3

Style direction + key decisions

We define finishes, colours, textures, and lighting direction. The goal is cohesive and maintainable — stylish, not fragile.

  • We deliver: finish palette + key recommendations.
  • Optional: quick alternatives for budget and availability.
Step 4

Shopping/spec list + implementation

You get a buyable list with alternatives and simple notes for implementation. Clear priorities, so it’s easy to execute without chaos.

  • We deliver: shopping/spec list + practical notes.
  • If you have a contractor: we can share simple guidance to keep it consistent.
