What you actually get

Every package is built around calm implementation: clear decisions, buyable items, and guidance you can actually follow.

  • Comfort-first planning: zones, flow, ergonomics, storage logic.
  • Style direction: key finishes, colours, textures — cohesive, not template.
  • Buyable shopping/spec list: with alternatives (to stay within budget and avoid out-of-stock pain).
  • Practical notes: priorities and implementation guidance (what to do first, what matters most).
  • Revision: one structured revision round is included. More iterations are available if needed.

Typical timeline

Most projects take 7–14 business days after we receive the brief and measurements. If you’re on a tight deadline, tell us — we’ll confirm what’s realistic.
